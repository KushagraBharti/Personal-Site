# Adaptive Agency

I care more about reaction time than position.

Most plans rot on contact. The skill is re-deriving fast, without making a thing of it. You drop the version of yourself that was right yesterday because that version is now a liability.

How I try to operate:

- think from primitives, not analogies to last quarter
- move before certainty turns into procrastination
- change direction without performing the change
- treat AI as exoskeleton, not identity

When intelligence becomes common infrastructure, the floor on answers drops. The premium relocates: context, taste, conviction, execution.

> A tool that absorbs your agency is just a tool that's using you.
