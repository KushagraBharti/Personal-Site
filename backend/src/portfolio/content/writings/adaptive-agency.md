# Adaptive Agency

Most plans are useful until they become identity. Agency is the ability to revise without waiting for permission, move without full certainty, and keep enough structure that speed does not become noise.

In a fast system, judgment matters less as a fixed opinion and more as update bandwidth. You need taste for which signal deserves attention, and enough flexibility to abandon a beautiful explanation when reality stops cooperating.

> intelligence is cheap only after access, context, and courage become common.
