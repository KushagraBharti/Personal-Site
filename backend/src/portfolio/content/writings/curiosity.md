# Curiosity

Curiosity is how I keep a project honest. Before I decide what to build, I try to understand what is actually happening, where the friction lives, and which assumptions are quietly carrying the most weight.

- Ask the obvious question twice.
- Follow the weird edge case.
- Treat confusion as signal, not failure.

The goal is not to know everything. The goal is to stay close enough to the problem that better questions keep appearing.
