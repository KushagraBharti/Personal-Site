# Aesthetic Judgment

Taste is not decoration. It is a form of compression: many small judgments collapsed into one decision that feels obvious after it exists.

Filmmaking made that concrete for me. A cut changes causality. Silence can carry more information than explanation. A frame can make the same fact feel cheap, sacred, dishonest, or inevitable.

Software has the same problem. The checklist can make something functional; it cannot tell you whether the thing has rhythm.
